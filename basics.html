Basic 1

  • Sit and Stand up on the ice
  • March Forward
  • Forward two foot glide
  • Dip
  • Forward Swizzles (6-8)
  • Beginning snowplow stop

Basic 2

  • Scooter Pushes
  • Forward one foot glide on both feet
  • Backward two foot glide
  • A consecutive forward Swizzle and a backward swizzle
  • Backward Swizzles (6-8)
  • Forward to Backward two foot turns
  • Moving Snowplow Stop

Basic 3

  • Beginning Forward Stroking
  • Forward Half-Swizzle Pumps (6-8 both directions)
  • Moving forward to backward two foot turns (both directions)
  • Beginning backward one foot glides
  • Backward snowplow stop
  • Forward Slalom

Basic 4

  • Forward Outside Edge on a circle (both directions)
  • Forward Inside Edge on a circle (both directions)
  • Forward crossovers (both directions)
  • Backward hald swizzle pumps on a circle (both directions)
  • Backward one-foot glides (right and left)
  • Beginning two foot spin

Basic 5

  • Backward outside edge on a circle
  • Backward inside edge on a cirlce
  • Backward crossovers (both directions)
  • Forward outside three turn (right and left)
  • Advanced two-foot spin (4 to 6)
  • Hockey stop, both directions

Basic 6

  • Forward inside three-turn (right and left)
  • Moving backward to forward two foot turn on a circle (both directions)
  • Backward stroking
  • Beginning one-foot spin (2-4 revs)
  • T-stops right and left (right and left)
  • Bunny hop
  • Forward Spiral (right and left)

DISCLAIMER: This list above does not include bonus skills!
